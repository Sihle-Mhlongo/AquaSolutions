Guidelines on how to purify water

1.Filter the Water:
Start by filtering the water to remove large particles and sediments.
You can use cloth, coffe filter, or a commercially available water filter to strain out impurities.

2.Boil the Water:
Boiling is an effective method to kill most of bacteria and viruses in water.
Bring the filtered water to a rolling boil for at least one minute to ensure it is safe to drink.

3.Use Chemical Disinfection:
If boiling is not possible, you can use chemical disfection method.
one commom method is to use liquid chlorine blench.
Add 8 drops (or 1/2 teaspoon) of unsure it is safe to drink .
Allow the water to cool before consuming.
